Clinical Connection

SLPs do not diagnose neurological lesions, but they must understand how brain structures support communication, cognition, hearing, swallowing, and motor speech. This helps connect client symptoms to possible underlying systems and supports effective assessment, documentation, referral, and intervention planning.
