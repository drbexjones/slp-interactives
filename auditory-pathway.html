Clinical Connection

Speech-language pathologists need to understand the auditory pathway because communication disorders may arise from problems at any point in this system.

  • Outer/Middle Ear: Conductive hearing loss
  • Cochlea: Sensorineural hearing loss
  • Auditory Nerve: Neural transmission deficits
  • Auditory Cortex: Central auditory processing difficulties
